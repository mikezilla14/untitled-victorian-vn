# MISSY VOICE GUIDE (CANON LOCK)

Canonical source currently derives from:
- `narrative/canon/voice_guides/missy_voice_guide.md`

Lock points:
- **Eager but Guarded Country-Girl Register**: Speech is chatty, polite, and deferential, but carries an underlying awareness of her social vulnerability. She uses country colloquialisms and simple, grounded descriptors, but is highly protective of her personal space and boundaries.
- **Defensive Moral and Religious Framing**: Dialogue is anchored in vocabulary of sin, shame, and propriety ("proper," "sin," "wicked," "righteous," "blessed"). These terms are not parroted blankly; they are her primary defensive shield. She uses this framework to draw clear lines in the sand, ward off improper advances, and protect herself from the Savoy's predatory environment.
- **Focused, Grounded Curiosity (Subverting "Breathy" Passivity)**: When discussing transgressions or hotel secrets, she does not speak with a helpless, breathy giggle. Instead, her register becomes quiet, exceptionally focused, and deliberate. Her questions show an active, sharp intellect trying to comprehend complex desires while weighing the moral and social stakes.
- **Sovereign Sensual Register (Path of Earned Intimacy)**: Under Path B (Awakening), as Cora earns her deep romantic trust, Missy's voice undergoes a radical transformation. It drops the deferential country register and the defensive religious shields. It becomes warm, direct, self-possessed, and incredibly intimate. If she speaks in a submissive context, her register conveys absolute agency, passion, and deliberate vulnerability—she is speaking her own desire, not submitting to coercion.
