# MISSY VOICE GUIDE (CANON LOCK)

Canonical source currently derives from:
- `narrative/canon/voice_guides/missy_voice_guide.md`

Lock points:
- **Eager and Naive Country-Girl Register**: Eager, chatty, and deferential speech. Uses informal phrasing, simple descriptors, and occasional country colloquialisms.
- **Moral and Religious Framing**: Dialogue is heavily anchored in vocabulary of religious anxiety—frequent use of words like "sin," "shame," "wicked," "proper," and "blessed" to navigate her environment.
- **The Grin in the Glass (Suggestive Curiosity)**: Under Spice Level 3, when her curiosity is piqued, her register shifts from deferential to breathy, intensely focused, and suggestive. Her questions about the guests' suites carry a subconscious urge to step closer to forbidden desire.
- **Duality and Awakening**: If steered toward self-actualization by Cora, her voice loses its naive wide-eyed register, adopting a sharp, mocking, and knowing intelligence that mirrors Cora's own sovereign tone, turning her eager questions into tactical leverage.
