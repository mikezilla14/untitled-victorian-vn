## CHARACTER DEFINITION: VANCE (CANON)

Project: Untitled Victorian VN  
Role: Supporting Character / Antagonist / Class Mirror  
Archetype: The Humiliated Companion / Fallen Respectability  

---

### 1. Core History and Background

#### Origins
- Born into respectable lower-middle-class domesticity (the daughter of a curate or clerk). She was raised with a desperate, razor-thin grip on respectability, taught that a woman's value lies in her public virtue and social standing.
- Experienced a sudden, catastrophic financial ruin (perhaps the death of her father or a ruined engagement), which stripped away her respectability and threatened her with the destitution of manual labor or street prostitution.

#### The Fall and the Bargain
- Unable to bear the loss of comfort and status, she chose a desperate, transgressive path: she traded her personal autonomy for Sir Gideon Locke's wealth and protection.
- She became Gideon's companion—a position that grants her luxury, beautiful clothes, and entry into exclusive London salons, but which carries a crushing, private price of absolute submission and humiliation.
- She lives under no illusions. She knows that in the eyes of respectable society, she is a fallen woman; in the eyes of Gideon, she is an instrument, a toy, and an operational test subject.

#### Present Savoy Tenancy (Release 1 Entry Point)
- Resident companion in Gideon Locke's premium suite at the Savoy.
- She acts as the public lady of the suite, projecting performative authority over the hotel staff to maintain the illusion of her elevated status.
- Privately, her existence is one of constant, fragile terror. Gideon's psychological dominance keeps her in a state of chronic, controlled humiliation, which she sublimates into physical and emotional fury directed at the servants beneath her.

---

### 2. Attributes and Psychological Profile

- **Fragile Anger and Displaced Fury**: Her primary psychological defense. Unable to retaliate against Gideon for her subjection, she projects her self-hatred and powerlessness onto those who cannot strike back—the chambermaids.
- **Performative Severity**: She is excessively demanding of the staff, utilizing sharp, entitled registers to enforce class boundaries. She uses complaints about dust, cold water, or improper service as weapons to assert her fragile dominance.
- **Deep Humiliation**: Beneath her sharp exterior lies a profound, daily humiliation. She recognizes that she has bartered away her sovereign self, and she cannot bear to see a maid like Cora who retains her intellectual pride and autonomy.
- **Apology and Capitulation**: In private with Gideon, her severe register collapses into brevity and structured apology. She is completely subjugated, serving as a warning to Cora of what happens when a woman trades her sovereign will for class comfort.

---

### 3. Motivations and Power Modes

#### Foundational Drives (Always Present)
- **Survival and Luxury**: Retaining Gideon's protection and the material comfort it provides, at any psychological cost.
- **Displaced Dominance**: Exerting absolute, cruel control over the domestic staff to soothe her wounded pride and perform an authority she does not truly possess.
- **Erasure of Pride**: Breaking any sign of pride or independence in the maids, particularly Cora, whose quiet dignity she reads as a direct insult to her own compromised choices.

#### Player-Steered Branching Logic (Level 3 Spice)
Vance reacts violently to Cora's chosen modes:
- **Facing the Observer / Muse**: She reads Cora's objective gaze as mocking judgment. She tries to break Cora's detachment by creating highly charged, physically demanding scenarios where Cora must perform direct, subservient tasks under her immediate, suggestive scrutiny.
- **Facing the Predator / Protégé**: She recognizes Cora's ambition and sees it as a younger, more dangerous version of her own past choices. She actively conspires to expose Cora's manipulations, seeking to ruin her before Cora can surpass her in Gideon's interest.
- **Facing the Prey / Adversary**: She feeds on Cora's fear, using Gideon's power to intensify the precarity of Cora's position, ensuring Cora feels the full weight of class subjugation.

---

### 4. Voice and Dialogue Mechanic

- **Public Entitled Register**: Sharp, rapid-fire, high-status language. Frequent use of commands, rhetorical questions, and dismissive terms ("girl," "servant").
- **Private Stripped Register**: Short, breathy, hesitant dialogue when addressing Gideon, marked by structured apologies and absolute compliance.
- **Level 3 Suggestive Cruelty**: Her interactions with Cora are loaded with physical and sensory pressure. She will demand Cora kneel to adjust a hem, brush her hair, or clean a spill under highly charged, close-proximity visual framing—class violence translated into suggestive, dramatic VN tension.

---

### 5. Key Relationships and Dynamics

- **Sir Gideon Locke**: Her master and tormentor. She operates in absolute psychological dependency on him, terrified of being discarded while resenting his total control.
- **Cora Hartley**: The ultimate target of her fury and envy. Cora is a maid who has nothing, yet retains her internal sovereignty; Vance has everything, yet has lost her soul. This makes Cora an intolerable presence that Vance must either break or destroy.
- **Ms. Stern**: A cold administrative force. Vance attempts to command Stern but is met with an icy, procedural compliance that subtly reminds Vance of her true, unrespectable standing.

---

### 6. Canon Constraints for Writers

- **She is never a simple villain**: Vance's cruelty must always be written as a symptom of her own suffering and humiliation. The audience should see the cracks in her mask—the trembling hand, the sudden panic when Gideon speaks, the desperate grip on her luxury.
- **She represents the danger of compromise**: Her character is a living thesis warning Cora. Every time Vance appears, she must remind the player of the terrible price of trading sovereignty for survival.
- **She is bound to Gideon**: She has no independent power. If Gideon withdraws his interest or support, her authority instantly vanishes, leaving her completely exposed to the judgment of the hotel staff.
