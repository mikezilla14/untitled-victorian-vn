# Savoy Guest Corridor: Background Visual Card

The grand upper guest hallway represents the opulent, silent, and highly protected territory of the Savoy's wealthy clientele. It stands in stark visual contrast to the plain, functional service corridors of the domestic staff.

## Design Parameters
*   **Aesthetic Tone**: Rich, heavy, quiet, and slightly suffocating. Gilded frames, deep reds, and dark woods absorb sound.
*   **Fidelity Markers**: Golden wainscoting trims, high-density floral crimson wallpaper patterns, detailed oil paintings (e.g. classical landscapes).
*   **Reusability Overlays**: Can be toggled with a dim night overlay (`overlay_guest_corridor_night.webp`) where sconces are lowered to a low flicker, creating high-contrast shadows.
