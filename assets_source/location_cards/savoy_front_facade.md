# Savoy Hotel Front Facade: Background Visual Card

The front facade of the Savoy Hotel represents late Victorian grand luxury. It acts as the transitional establishing shot, bridging the gritty streets of London with the opulence inside.

## Design Parameters
*   **Aesthetic Tone**: Glistening, majestic, but desaturated and slightly somber, reflecting late Victorian overcast rainy days.
*   **Perspective**: Low-angle shot looking up slightly at the grand entryway, making the hotel feel looming and architecturally significant.
*   **Baked-in Details to Avoid**: Avoid highly localized modern signs, specific year engravings (beyond 1891), or distinct walking pedestrians in the base image.
