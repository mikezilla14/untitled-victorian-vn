# Savoy Servants' Quarters: Background Visual Card

The attic servants' quarters represent the private, cramped, and cold environment where Cora and Missy sleep and where Cora drafts her forbidden manuscript late at night.

## Design Parameters
*   **Aesthetic Tone**: Intimate, vulnerable, fragile, and quiet. The attic ceiling slopes heavily, suggesting confinement.
*   **Fidelity Markers**: Iron bedframes, patchwork quilts, simple wood drawers, ceramic basin, dormer window panes.
*   **Layering Overlays**: A candle glow overlay (`overlay_quarters_candle.webp`) can be applied to illuminate Cora's bedside table when she writes.
