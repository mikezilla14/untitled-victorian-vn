# Savoy Laundry Room: Background Visual Card

The laundry room is a subterranean space characterized by physical exhaustion and heavy industrial domestic labor. The visual focus is on damp stone, massive copper tubs, and rising condensation.

## Design Parameters
*   **Aesthetic Tone**: Exhausting, humid, and heavy. Wet stones and copper vats cast cool, damp shadows.
*   **Fidelity Markers**: Steam coils, copper pipes, wet washboards, stacked wooden laundry crates.
