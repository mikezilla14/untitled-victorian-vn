# Cora's Writing Desk: Background Visual Card

Cora's desk is an intimate close-up plate showing her raw writing environment: the desk surface, the flickering candlelight, and her forbidden manuscript pages.

## Design Parameters
*   **Aesthetic Tone**: Intensely focused, high-contrast, dark, and literary.
*   **HUD Compatibility**: The desk composition must leave the left edge clear to align with the persistent left sidebar portrait/stats HUD, while aligning the empty/full inkwell sprite directly near the visual quill pen.
*   **Layering Overlays**: A candlelight flicker overlay (`overlay_desk_flicker.webp`) simulates dynamic lighting changes as the player debates transgressive manuscript choices.
