# MS. STERN VOICE GUIDE (CANON LOCK)

Canonical source currently derives from:
- `main-game/canon/voice_guides/stern_voice_guide.md`

Lock points:
- **Procedural and Administrative Vocabulary**: Speaks with formal, clipped, and clinical language. Uses passive voice and dry institutional phrasing to execute authority.
- **The Sublimated Dominant Subtext**: Her dialogue utilizes quiet commands and pauses rather than volume or physical threats. She dominates conversational spacing, using slow, methodical observations (e.g., inspecting collars, checking schedules) to assert control.
- **Double Meaning/Suggestive Warnings**: Under Spice Level 3, her statements carry dual meanings. When she warns the maids about "safety," "keeping one's dress clean," or "avoiding the gaze of guests," her dialogue carries a highly charged forensic awareness of both the dark appeal of transgression and the ruinous price of its discovery.
- **Controlled Sublimation**: She never speaks with overt emotion, anger, or moral self-righteousness. Her severity is written strictly as professional duty and cynical working-class armor.
